Scientific Employee / PhD Candidate - Alfred Wegener Institute / Bremen University

09/08/2020 – 18/11/2023, Bremerhaven, Germany

PhD thesis “Optical observations and distribution modeling of gelatinous zooplankton in the Arctic Ocean”

Group: Helmholtz Young Investigator Group ARJEL (2019-2025): Arctic jellies: Impact of gelatinous zooplankton communities on changing Arctic ecosystems

Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Charlotte Havermans

Description: Leveraging extensive datasets and optical data from recent expeditions, my PhD research focuses on the diversity, distribution, and abundance of gelatinous zooplankton across Arctic ecosystems.
